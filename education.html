MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

Learning Never Ends

– Primary School –

2012-2017

SK Seri Tasek — I began my academic journey at Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Tasek, where I built a strong foundation in basic literacy, numeracy, and communication skills. During these years, I actively participated in classroom activities and school events, which helped me develop confidence and teamwork. In the national UPSR examination, I achieved a result of 1A, 3B, and 1C — a stepping stone that motivated me to improve and continue striving for academic growth.

– Secondary School –

2018-2022

SMK Simpang Empat — I continued my studies at SMK Simpang Empat, where I was a dedicated student and served as the assistant class leader in Year 6. My involvement in co-curricular activities, including poetry competitions and athletics, helped me develop leadership qualities and time management. Academically, I maintained consistent performance and successfully scored 3A, 3B, 1C, 1D in the SPM examination. This experience shaped my discipline and prepared me for the challenges of higher education.

– College Matriculation –

2023-2024

Matriculation Pulau Pinang — After secondary school, I pursued a Foundation in Account at College Matriculation Pulau Pinang. The rigorous academic environment taught me how to manage complex assignments, conduct scientific research, and collaborate effectively in group projects. I particularly enjoyed subjects such as Account and Mathematics, which sharpened my analytical thinking. I graduated with a CGPA of 3.17, a reflection of my dedication and ability to balance academic pressure with personal growth.

– University –

2024-2027

UiTM Puncak Perdana — Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Information Science (Hons.) at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Puncak Perdana. My programme specializes in Information Systems Management, where I have explored various topics such as metadata, information retrieval, database management, and UI/UX design. I am actively involved in group projects and faculty activities, which enhance my collaboration and presentation skills. This degree is equipping me with both theoretical and practical knowledge to thrive in the digital information industry.
